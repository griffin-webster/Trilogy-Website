Hunger Games

What is this trilogy about?

This trilogy includes many interesting features. To name a few, it has:

  • Adventure
  • Action
  • A tyrannical government
  • Heartbreak

Though it is difficult to sum up the story (and I don't want to spoil it for you), this trilogy features characters such as Finnick, Katniss, and Peeta entering into combat against each other in a game-like environment with deadly consequences. However, they eventually discover a mutual enemy: the government.

There are four three books in this series, and the books have been adapted into movies. The books (written by Suzanne Collins) include:

  1. The Hunger Games
  2. Catching Fire
  3. Mockingjay

There is much more to discover about this great trilogy. If you would like to learn more, I would advise you to read the books. If this task is too daunting, you may utilize either of the links below.
